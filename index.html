How It Works

Three simple steps to transform any material into practice questions

1

Customize Your Prompt

Visit our Prompt Generator, set your difficulty level and number of questions, then paste your study material (textbook chapter, notes, research paper).

2

Generate with Any LLM

Copy the complete prompt and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any LLM. The AI will generate perfectly formatted questions ready to use.

3

Load & Practice

Copy the JSON output from the LLM, paste it into Muscle Memory, and start your quiz. Questions are randomized and tracked for optimal learning.
